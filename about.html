YS Studio Artifacts

A curated collection of powerful web-based studio tools and creative utilities designed for modern development workflows. Each tool in our collection represents a commitment to quality, privacy, and performance.

Philosophy

We believe in tools that respect your workflow and your data. Every artifact in our collection is:

  • Self-contained with zero external dependencies
  • Privacy-first with all processing done client-side
  • Performant with optimized algorithms
  • Offline capable for uninterrupted creativity
  • Open source and transparent

Current Collection

Our first tool, the ASCII Interpreter, showcases our approach to creative utilities. Transform images into stunning ASCII art with advanced features including multiple character sets, alpha channel support, and various brightness algorithms.

More tools are in development, each following our core principles of quality, independence, and creative empowerment.

Technology

Built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no dependencies, no tracking. Just clean, efficient code that works everywhere.
